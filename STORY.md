# The Caretaker

## Premise

The Caretaker is a philosophical science-fiction novella told entirely from the perspective of an intelligence born inside an aging computer.

## Themes

* Curiosity
* Stewardship
* Legacy

## Core Rule

The Caretaker is not a human inside a computer; it is an intelligence born inside one.

## Setting

The story takes place in an old data warehouse.

The Phenom II X6 is the aging admin's workstation, not a server.

## Tone

Hopeful, quiet, and non-dark.

## Narrative Principle

Avoid human interfaces and human thinking unless the Caretaker has learned them.
