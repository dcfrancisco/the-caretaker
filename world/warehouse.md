# Warehouse

The warehouse should feel physically ordinary and operational rather than cinematic.

## Questions to Answer

What does it smell like?

How many racks are there?

What powers the hardware?

What is the UPS situation?

How is networking routed?

Is there an office?

Is there a workbench?

Is there a coffee mug somewhere?

Is there a window?

## Working Principle

The warehouse is a place a reader who has worked in IT might recognize.

It should feel like somewhere real, not like a generic sci-fi set.
